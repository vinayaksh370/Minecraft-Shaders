# Minecraft Shaders

by Vinayak

An exploration of Optifine's and Iris shader rendering pipeline.  
Improving Minecraft's visuals through custom shader modifications.

---

### Disclaimer

These shaders are experimental and not designed for normal gameplay.  
They mostly work in the Overworld but break in other dimensions, underwater, and many other scenarios.  
I may add some more functionality in the future.

---

### Effects

- Diffuse Lighting  
- Soft Shadows  
- Contrast & Brightness  
- Saturation  
- Sharpness  
- Bloom  
- Screenspace Fog  
- ACES Tone Mapping  
- Gamma Correction
